About Aldersgate College

Transforming Lives
Since 1965

Aldersgate College, Inc. was founded to develop the power of reason, to liberate people from ignorance, penury, and hopelessness — empowering them to commence their own journey for social and economic transformation and spiritual salvation.

Rooted in the United Methodist tradition and driven by a vision of global excellence, ACI offers affordable, flexible, and collaborative educational programs using systems that comply with international standards of quality, professional licensure, and practice.

Academic Excellence

Ranked nationally and recognized by the International Association of Universities

Methodist values forming morally upright and spiritually awakened graduates

Social Relevance

Community extension programs reaching beyond the campus walls

Global Reach

Career pathways through a network of transnational institutions & guilds

A Legacy of Transformation

From a small Junior College in Solano, Nueva Vizcaya to a nationally recognized institution — Aldersgate College has spent six decades shaping ethical leaders, compassionate servants, and global citizens.
